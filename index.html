About This Database

This Nursing Diagnosis Database was built using content extracted and normalized from the Ackley and Ladwig’s Nursing Diagnosis Handbook: An Evidence-Based Guide to Planning Care 13th Edition. The data includes diagnoses, definitions, related factors, characteristics, outcomes, and interventions, which serve as the foundation for our AI system in generating Nursing Care Plans (NCPs). A shortened and structured version of the book used, which the pages are based on, for extraction can be accessed here.

This site showcases the knowledge base we are working with and is part of an ongoing thesis study entitled "Automated Nursing Care Plan Creation Based on NANDA-I, NIC, and NOC Standards Using Pre-Trained Transformer Models". You can watch a quick walkthrough of our system demo here. If you are a nursing student, we invite you to contribute by submitting your own Nursing Care Plans through our submission form. Your participation will directly help improve the system and support this research.
